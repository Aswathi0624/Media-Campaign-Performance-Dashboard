#  Media Campaign Performance Dashboard: End-to-End Analytics using Power BI, Python & SQL

## 📌 Project Overview
Developed an end-to-end analytics solution to analyze and visualize media campaign performance across multiple marketing channels. The project leverages **Python for data preprocessing**, **SQL for data management and querying**, and **Power BI for interactive dashboard development** to provide actionable business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives
* Monitor overall campaign performance and key business metrics.
* Analyze campaign effectiveness across different marketing channels.
* Evaluate customer segments and conversion performance.
* Identify revenue drivers and factors influencing ROI.
* Provide strategic recommendations through interactive dashboards.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Power BI** – Dashboard development and data visualization
* **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
* **SQL** – Data storage, querying, and aggregation
* **Excel/CSV** – Dataset source

## 📂 Dataset Features
* Campaign_ID
* Campaign_Type
* Target_Audience
* Duration
* Channel_Used
* Impressions
* Clicks
* Leads
* Conversions
* Revenue
* Acquisition_Cost
* ROI
* Customer_Segment
* Date

## 🔄 Project Workflow

### 1. Data Collection
Collected media campaign data containing campaign performance, customer segments, and marketing channel information.

### 2. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing (Python)
* Handled missing values and inconsistent date formats.
* Converted data types and standardized fields.
* Removed duplicates and validated records.
* Exported cleaned dataset for analysis.

### 3. Data Management and Analysis (SQL)
* Imported cleaned data into SQL database.
* Performed aggregations and business queries.
* Calculated KPIs and campaign metrics.

### 4. Dashboard Development (Power BI)
Built a professional three-page interactive dashboard with filters and drill-down capabilities.

# 📈 Dashboard Pages

## 📊 Page 1 – Media Campaign Performance Dashboard

**Purpose:** Executive overview of campaign performance.

**Key Visualizations**

* KPI Cards (Impressions, Acquisition Cost, Revenue, Clicks, Conversions, ROI)
* Revenue Contribution by Channel
* Revenue by Campaign Type
* Revenue Trend Over Time
* Campaign Type Distribution
* Top Campaign Revenue Summary

## 📈 Page 2 – Campaign Performance Analysis

**Purpose:** Analyze campaign effectiveness and channel performance.

**Key Visualizations**

* Acquisition Cost by Channel
* Revenue by Customer Segment
* Conversions by Channel
* Clicks Trend per Channel
* Campaign Type Ranking Over Time

## 💡 Page 3 – Customer Insights & Recommendations

**Purpose:** Generate business insights and strategic recommendations.

**Key Visualizations**

* Channel Performance by Customer Segment
* Revenue Driver Analysis (Decomposition Tree)
* Impressions Trend Over Time
* Conversion Rate by Campaign Type
* Factors Influencing High ROI (Key Influencers)

## 📊 Key Business KPIs
* Total Impressions
* Total Acquisition Cost
* Total Revenue
* Total Clicks
* Total Conversions
* Return on Investment (ROI)

## 🔍 Key Insights
* Identified top-performing marketing channels and campaign types.
* Determined customer segments contributing the highest revenue.
* Analyzed conversion efficiency across campaign types.
* Evaluated factors significantly impacting campaign ROI.
* Enabled data-driven recommendations for optimizing marketing investments.

## 🚀 Business Impact

* Improved visibility into campaign performance metrics.
* Enabled faster and data-driven decision-making.
